NutritionTrak — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-20

This Privacy Policy explains how NutritionTrak (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the “Service”). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

  • Account & Contact Data — name, email address, and optional profile details you provide.
  • App Activity & Content — diet logs, goals, nutrition entries, favorites, and preferences you save in the app.
  • Device & Usage — device model, OS version, app version, crash logs, and basic analytics (e.g., screens visited, session length).
  • Optional Integrations — if you connect third‑party services (e.g., fitness trackers), we process data strictly to provide the integration.

Sensitive health-related entries are used only to power in‑app features you choose (e.g., tracking nutrients, generating recommendations).

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core features (logging foods, tracking nutrients, exporting data).
  • Improve reliability and performance, including debugging and crash analytics.
  • Personalize the experience (units, goals, reminders).
  • Communicate updates and respond to support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with service providers that process information on our behalf (e.g., analytics, cloud storage) under confidentiality and data‑processing agreements. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

Your Choices & Controls

  • Access & Correction — You may request a copy of your data and ask us to correct inaccuracies.
  • Portability — Export your entries (e.g., CSV/PDF) where supported in the app.
  • Deletion — Use the “Delete My Data” request form below or contact us at zub165@yahoo.com.
  • Opt‑out — You can disable non‑essential analytics in your device settings where applicable.

In‑App Data Deletion

Inside the app, navigate to Settings → Privacy → Delete Account/Data to erase locally stored entries and unlink cloud backups (if enabled). Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

If you signed in with a third‑party account (e.g., Apple/Google), you may also need to revoke access in your provider’s account settings.

Submit a Data Deletion Request

Email Support

Clicking “Delete My Data” opens your email client with a pre‑filled request so we can verify ownership and process deletion securely.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the Service and for legitimate business or legal purposes. You can request deletion at any time.

Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Security

We apply administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

International Transfers

If you access the Service from outside the country where our servers or providers are located, your information may be processed in other countries that may have different data protection laws. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA/CPRA)

  • GDPR (EU/UK) — right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection.
  • CCPA/CPRA (California) — right to know, delete, correct, and opt‑out of sharing/sale (we do not sell data).

To exercise any right, email zub165@yahoo.com and state your request and the email used in the app.
